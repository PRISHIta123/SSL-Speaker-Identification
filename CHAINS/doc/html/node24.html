SOLO

The raw recordings of the SOLO condition consisted of a mono file per speaker and microphone. Each mono file contained the whole read material (short fables and sentences). Speech samples of the short fables have been obtained by editing out dysfluencies or portions of speech not adhering to the above criteria. Pause durations are thus largely byproducts of the file trimming procedure, and do not represent actual pause behavior.
